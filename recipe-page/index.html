LOADED BAKED SWEET POTATOES

This recipe is inspired by the viral TikTok bowl with sweet potato, cottage cheese, honey, ground beef, and avocado—but turned into a loaded baked sweet potato with a spicy hot honey drizzle. It’s high protein, delicious, and anything but boring. Perfect for a cozy and satisfying weeknight dinner.

PREP TIME: 10 Minutes COOK TIME: 55 Minutes

TOTAL TIME: 1h 10 Minutes YIELDS: 3 to 4 yields

INGREDIENTS

SWEET POTATOES

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes
  • Olive or avocado oil, for rubbing
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Butter, for serving

GROUND BEEF

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive or avocado oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup beef broth

HOT HONEY

  • 1/4 cup hot honey
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes, to taste
  • 1/2 tablespoon vinegar (white or apple cider)

TOPPINGS

  • cottage cheese, as needed
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced or mashed

INSTRUCTIONS

ROAST THE SWEET POTATOES

  1. Wash and dry the sweet potatoes.
  2. Carefully pierce each sweet potato several times with a fork or sharp knife (cuts don’t need to be deep).
  3. Rub each potato with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Air fry at 400°F for 45 to 50 minutes, flipping them after 30 minutes, until fork-tender and skins are crisp.

COOK THE GROUND BEEF (MAKE WHILE POTATOES ARE ROASTING)

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef, breaking it up with a spatula, and cook for 6 to 8 minutes until browned.
  4. Air fry at 400°F for 45 to 50 minutes, flipping them after 30 minutes, until fork-tender and skins are crisp.
  5. Pour in beef broth and simmer for 3 to 5 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the meat.

MAKE THE HOT HONEY (MAKE WHILE POTATOES ARE ROASTING)

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.

ASSEMBLY

  1. Carefully slice open each sweet potato.
  2. Add a pat of butter, season with salt and pepper, then mash and fluff the flesh with a fork so the butter melts in and the texture is creamy.
  3. Top each potato with a scoop of ground beef.
  4. Add a generous spoonful of cottage cheese.
  5. Finish with sliced or mashed avocado.
  6. Drizzle with hot honey and serve warm.
